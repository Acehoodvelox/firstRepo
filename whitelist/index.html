Private Presale

Whitelist and Presale Information

Dear Jadeite Community,

Since launching Jadeite Token, our team has experienced overwhelming support from the community. We would like to take this opportunity to thank every single person who have helped us grow and spread the word through Telegram, Twitter, Reddit and other social media platforms.

We are happy to announce that our whitelist approval process for Private Presale started on 29th July, and here you can find all the required informations.

As always, please feel free to join our official Telegram channel where our team is always ready and willing to help out where needed.

When your application has been processed you will receive a reply from whitelist@jadeite.site to the email address provided in the submission form to let you know if you have been approved (Make sure to also check your spam or junk mail folder).

 

WHITELIST SUBMISSION

Presale Information

When will the Presale start?
Presale Start: August 15, 2021, Sunday, 19:00 UTC
Presale End: August 25, 2021, Wednesday, 19:00 UTC

What are the min and max contribution?
Minimum contribution amount: 0,1 BNB
Maximum contribution amount: 2 BNB

What are the caps?
Soft Cap: 200 BNB
Hard Cap: 400 BNB

The allocation set aside for the Presale
Total allocated: 10,4 % from the total supply (10.400.000.000 JDT)

Presale Rate
Whitelist Private presale Rate: 26,000,000 per 1 BNB
DxSale Public presale Rate: 20.000.000 per 1 BNB
Pancakeswap rate: 15,500,000 per 1 BNB

1 JDT = 0.00000003846153846153 BNB (whitelist presale rate)
1 JDT = 0.00000005 BNB (DxSale presale rate)
1 JDT = 0.00000006451612903225 BNB (PancakeSwap rate)

DxSale min and max contribution?
Minimum contribution amount: 0,1 BNB
Maximum contribution amount: 5 BNB

What will we need to submit for Whitelist?

We require the following:

  1. Name
  2. Country of residence
  3. Email address
  4. Telegram username (optional)
  5. How much BNB are you planning on contribute
  6. Binance Smart Chain wallet address *

*Please note that we require the wallet address and not the seed phrase or the private key. No one from our team will ever request you the seed phrase or the private key.

How can I enter the presale if was whitelisted?

 If you receive confirmation that you are on the whitelist you will be able to participate in the presale, we will announce the link for our Presale dApp on Telegram and also via email.

Can I contribute again on DxSale Presale if I already contributed on Private Presale?

Yes everyone will still be able to buy in on DxSale presale.

Is your Presale going to be first come first serve?

As our Presale hardcap is set to 400 BNB, there will be an element of first come first serve. We can’t guarantee that every person that will be on our whitelist will get his spot.

When I can claim my tokens?

In order to claim your tokens presale needs to be finalized and soft cap or hardcap reached

What if Softcap is not reached?

When the presale is finished and it hasn’t reached the softcap, all early investors will be able to withdraw back the funds from the crowdsale smart contract!

What happens if I send my funds in after the hardcap has been reached?

Any funds received once our hardcap has been reached will be refunded after the Presale has finished.
